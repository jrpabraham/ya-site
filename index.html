Our Story

David and Leyanne first crossed paths in April 2017. David had a crush on Leyanne but it wasn't until January 2019 that the spark between them ignited into a flame of romance.

David and Leyanne have since shared countless moments of joy, laughter, and adventure. From exploring new destinations to trying different foods at new restaurants, each experience added to their love story. Through the ups and downs, navigating the challenges of a global pandemic and conquering the trials of a long-distance relationship while David pursued a master's degree in Florida, their love only grew stronger.

In November 2023, David asked Leyanne to be his forever and, with tears of joy and hearts full of love, they embraced the next chapter of their story—an engagement that marked the beginning of a lifetime together. David and Leyanne will continue to grow in love as they aspire to travel the world, experience new adventures, and chase their dreams together.

FAQ

What is the dress code?

The dress code for our event is formal. Ladies should wear a floor-length evening gown or your most dressy cocktail dress. Gentlemen should wear a suit with a tie or bow tie. Please reserve navy blue for our bridal party.


Can I bring a plus one?

Due to limited space, we can only accommodate those guests formally invited on your wedding invitation. If you received an invitation for a plus one, they would be listed under your name when you RSVP.


Can I bring children?

We love your little ones but due to our limited space, we respectfully request you only bring children included in the invitation.


How long does it take to get from the church to the reception?

Driving from the church to the reception venue should take approximately 15 without traffic.


Where do you recommend guests stay overnight?

For your convenience, a block of rooms have been reserved at the Home2 Suites by Hilton Hayward at a discounted rate. Please book a room in our hotel block using the link in the RSVP form.


I have other questions, how do I contact you?

Please send us an e-mail at annedavidpapa@gmail.com. We look forward to having you as part of our special day.
